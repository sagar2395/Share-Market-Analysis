# Dashboard

The landing screen — a one-glance health check before you dig in.

## What's on it
- **Three stat cards:** total **Invested**, current **Market value**, and **Unrealised
  P&L** (₹ and %) across your whole portfolio. Green = in profit, red = in loss.
- **Watchlist movers:** your watchlist sorted by the day's % change (biggest gainers at
  the top, losers at the bottom) — a fast read on where the action is.

## Use it
- **Click any row** in the movers table to open that stock in **Analyze**.
- Glance at P&L to decide whether anything needs attention (near a stop/target?), then
  switch to **Portfolio** for detail or **Analyze** to study a name.

## Notes
- Totals come from your manually-entered holdings (see [Portfolio](portfolio.md)); the
  movers come from your [Watchlist](watchlist.md). Empty lists show a hint to add items.
- Values use latest/EOD prices — the right cadence for a 2-week-to-6-month horizon.
